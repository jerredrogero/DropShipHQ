Privacy Policy

Last updated:

1. Information We Collect

In addition to the information provided during account creation, we collect and store the following data:

  • User Profile Information: Including but not limited to username, email address, and subscription details.
  • Order Information: Details of orders placed through our platform, including date, buying group, account, order number, tracking number, product, merchant, card used, cost, reimbursement status, and cash back amount.
  • Subscription Information: Current plan, start date, end date (if applicable), and order count.
  • Payment Information: We do not store full credit card details. For users on paid plans, we store a Stripe customer ID which is used for recurring payments and plan management.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our service, including managing your subscription and orders.
  • To process payments and prevent fraud.
  • To notify you about changes to your subscription or our service.
  • To provide customer support and respond to your requests.
  • To monitor usage of our service and improve our offerings.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

We may use aggregated, anonymized data for analytical purposes and to improve our service.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent.
  • With service providers who assist us in providing our services (e.g., Stripe for payment processing). These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information.
  • If required by law or legal process.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of BuyingGroupPro, our users, or others.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect information about you from loss, theft, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You may also have additional rights depending on your jurisdiction. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section below.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. If you close your account, we will retain your information for a period of time to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, prevent fraud, and enforce our agreements.

Subscription history and order data may be retained for a longer period for analytical and business intelligence purposes, but in an anonymized format that cannot be linked back to individual users.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may change this privacy policy from time to time. We will post any privacy policy changes on this page and update the "Last updated" date at the top. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@buyinggrouppro.com.
